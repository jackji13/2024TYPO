50 TYPEFACES RESEARCH

There are two parts to this visual study project. Part A requires cutting nine 3" x 3" text blocks from black and white publications and arranging them together in a grid. Part B involves making a collage using colored text from publications. Both parts should be 9" x 9" in size and glued onto an illustration board.
