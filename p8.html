50 TYPEFACES RESEARCH

This is a weekly visual study of 50 different typefaces. Each week, five typefaces were studied by creating a design using the letter "g" in these five typefaces. Along with the "g" design, a pangram featuring "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog" was also created. The study spans a total of ten weeks.
