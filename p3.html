TYPE TIRO GK5

This project strated with the creation of nine thumbnails featuring two letters and a number: "k", "g", and "5". The typefaces were Adobe Caslon Regular, Folio Bold and Kuenstler Script Black. A chosen design was sketched in an 18" x 18" newsprint using black marker. It was then digitized in InDesign with the same fonts and printed. The hand-drawn and printed designs were compared, and adjustments were marked.
