TYPEFACE DESIGN

Create your own typeface by cutting out historical letterforms from black paper first. Name your typeface and provide usage recommendations. Focus on the forms and spaces characteristic of the Phoenician, Greek, and Roman alphabets. Arrange four letterforms within an 8-inch square grid, allotting a 4-inch square for each. Use the grid to size elements correctly but exclude it from your final presentation. Adjust and refine sizes using scanning and Illustrator for your final design. Introduce your typeface to the design community through mail, web, and app. Design a accordion-fold promotional brochure with an envelope.
