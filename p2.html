AND And and &

This project begins by locating and cutting out various forms of "AND, And, and, &" from publications. Altivo Medium, a sans-serif font, has been selected to create nine visual studies. Each study must be contained within a 6" x 6" box, using only black and white colors. The typefaces should not overlap or be deformed.
