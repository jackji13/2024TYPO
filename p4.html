TYPE TALK

This project focused on creating designs for three attitudes: "airy, flowing, quiet," "madness, frenzy, disorder," and "exquisite, elegant, charming." Lorem ipsum text was overlapped, repeated, enlarged, and reduced in various typefaces to match these moods. The designs, restricted to black and white, were presented in 8.5" x 11" format. For part 2, choose a date and make a design that mataches its attitude.
